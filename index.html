PLACES MUST TRAVEL

Visited in July, 2023

Belize is a beautiful place in Central America with its beaches and ruins. Belize is special because it is one of the 7 countries you can see the mayan ruins. It is also one of the best places to see ruins. I would recomend going in the fall though because it gets HOT in the summer and you might have to walk for sometime before reaching the ruins.

The Hunger Games: Mockingjay Part 1 is a 2014 American dystopian action film that is the third installment in The Hunger Games film series.
